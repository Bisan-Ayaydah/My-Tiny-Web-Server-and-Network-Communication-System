Summary: Cache Terms from RFC 2616

  • Cache-Control: Specifies caching behavior (public, private, no-cache).
  • Expires: Date/time after which response is considered outdated.
  • Last-Modified: Timestamp indicating last update of the resource.
  • ETag: Unique identifier for a specific version of a resource.
  • Age: How long a response has been cached since its creation.
